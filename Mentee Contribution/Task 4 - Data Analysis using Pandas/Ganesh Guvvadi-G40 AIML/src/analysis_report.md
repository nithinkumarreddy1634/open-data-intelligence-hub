## 9. Key Insights

1. Saturday generated the highest total bill amount.
2. Sunday also contributed significantly to revenue.
3. Average tips vary across different days.
4. Most customers visited during dinner time.
5. Male customers appeared more frequently than female customers.
6. Smoking and non-smoking customers are both represented.
7. Larger party sizes generally resulted in higher bills.
8. The dataset was clean and suitable for analysis.
